ABOUT

I design mobile and web applications for startups and small businesses.

I focus on creating applications that are simple, usable, and enjoyable. I have experience designing and building products, from paper to production.

Self-taught designer. Started as a designer at Vimov (known for WeatherHD, featured by Apple as the best weather app), worked as a freelancer, then cofounded asknative.

Past 3+ years, I worked as a product designer & web developer. Shaping the asknative experience through designing the iOS, Android, and Web apps; while building the front & back end. Also worked with several clients to design their mobile and web applications.

My process usually involves user research, information architecture, interaction design, visual and motion design, prototyping, and sometimes coding.

Passionate about solving problems through design, and always curious to learn something new every day.

Through projects that I take, I try to leave the world a little better than I found it.

You can contact me at me@seifsallam.com, and you can find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, Medium, and GitHub.
